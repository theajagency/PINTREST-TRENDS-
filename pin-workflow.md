## GARMENT CLASSIFICATION LOCK

Always identify garment type first.

**Step 1: Category**
- dress
- top
- skirt
- set
- bottoms

**Step 2: Silhouette priority**
- If the garment continues past the hips as one piece, classify as dress
- If it is one continuous full-body piece, classify as dress
- If unclear, default to dress over top

**Step 3: Add style after category**

Correct:
- cami mini dress
- halter maxi dress
- floral bodycon dress
- lace cami dress
- halter top
- cami top
- tennis skirt outfit

Incorrect:
- cami top if the item is actually a dress
- halter top if the item is actually a dress

**Step 4: Never downgrade garment type**
- Dress never becomes top because of straps or neckline
- Garment type comes before strap type, print, trim, or aesthetic
