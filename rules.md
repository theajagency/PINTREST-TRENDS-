# Search Rules & Guardrails

## DO Search
All keywords listed in `keywords.md`. Search every single one, every day.

---

## DO NOT Search (Avoid This Week)
These topics are off the list entirely. Do not search them, do not include them in reports, and do not surface pins that are primarily about these:

- DIY
- sewing
- crochet pattern
- nails
- makeup
- hair
- food
- prom (broad) — see exception below

---

## Prom Exception
Only include prom-related results if the item being pinned is **clearly and specifically promwear** (formal gown, prom dress). If a search term like "mini dress" surfaces prom content, skip those pins. Do not search "prom" as a standalone keyword.

---

## What to Bring Back in Every Report

1. **What looks UP** — keywords gaining momentum, high activity, strong 2026 signals
2. **What looks STABLE** — keywords holding steady, consistent saves, no dramatic change
3. **What looks WEAK** — keywords losing volume, old content dominating, declining signals
4. **Strong related terms Pinterest suggests** — note any auto-suggested or related searches Pinterest surfaces for a keyword (these can become next week's keywords)

---

## Report Scope
- Do not include commentary on DIY versions, patterns, tutorials, or how-to content
- Focus on wearable fashion: actual outfits, styling, items to buy or recreate
- Note seasonal relevance where applicable (e.g., "likely climbing due to festival season")
